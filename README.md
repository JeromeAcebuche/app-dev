# My Favorite Movie: Madea Goes to Jail

## Overview
After a high-speed car chase, the world-famous Madea ends up behind bars. While she navigates life in prison with her usual flair, a district attorney finds himself caught between his career and a former friend who needs help.

## Why I Like It
* **Humor:** Tyler Perry’s performance as Madea is consistently hilarious.
* **Themes:** It balances comedy with serious messages about redemption and forgiveness.
* **Dynamics:** The interactions between Madea and the other inmates provide some of the best moments.

### Key Characters
1. Madea
2. Joshua Hardaway
3. Candace "Candy" Washington

> "I’m a grandmother, I’m a lady, and I’m a thug!" — Madea
